Kanyakumari


Beauty


It is also one of those rare countries that have their shores shared between three great seas - the Bay of Bengal, the Arabian Sea, and the Indian Ocean. And the confluence of these three seas can be witnessed in Kanyakumari. This unique geographical phenomenon has made this little town in the southernmost tip of mainland India as one of the most significant destinations in any religious or pleasure trips that one seeks to undertake in this country. The main appeal of this place is in standing at the tip of the country and witnessing the most beautiful sunrise in the mornings, and the most soothing sunset in the evenings.
