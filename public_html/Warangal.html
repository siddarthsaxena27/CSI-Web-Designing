Warangal


Beauty


From the magnificent Kakatiya Thoranam, to the intricately carved Thousand Pillar Temple and the magnificent Warangal Fort, heritage and culture is not something that Warangal is short of. Today, Warangal sits proudly in the heart of India's youngest state, Telangana and enriches its beauty with its splendour and grandeur. Apart from the physical attractions, it is the sheer beauty and enriching greenery that draws people to this holistic destination.
