Chennai


Beauty


It has a very different and unique culture when compared with the rest of the country. Chennai is famous for its "Carnatic" music style, which is set deeply into the heritage of the country. Furthermore, it is home to the Pallava temple, a UNESCO world heritage site, the beautiful temples of Kanchipuram and the regal Fort St.George. Visitors are drawn to the this place for its unique food, and varied culture.
