Why Travel?

The human race is considered to be one of the most advanced species in existence. But there is one characteristic of us human beings, that tends to reduce our efficiency.Fatigue from the monotonity of our daily lives can sometimes get overwhelming. Travelling is often considered as one of the most theraputic experiences for a human mind.The prospect of venturing far from home, to explore distinct lands and know their cultures is exciting, to say the least. Travel is something that gets our pulse racing, and sets our mind on edge. It makes us forget any worldly problems and just immerse ourselves fully in the experience. Perhaps then, its been rightly said: "We travel not to escape life, but for life to not escape us".
