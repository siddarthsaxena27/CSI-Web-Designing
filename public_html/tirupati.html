Tirupati


Beauty


It is home to the Sri Venkateswara Temple, which sees the highest influx of devotees among any other in the country. The town itself is located in the foothills, where as the temple is located on top of the Tirumala hills, which have seven peaks, popularly referred to as "Sapthagiri", symbolizing the seven hoods Adisesha, the serpent under whose hoods Vishnu resides in Vaikuntha.Tirupati is considered a major holy city for Hinduism and receives a significant amount of pilgrimage traffic. It is referred to as the "Spiritual Capital of Andhra Pradesh". Many attractions here are geared towards religious tourism.
